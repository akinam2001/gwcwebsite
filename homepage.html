About Us


“Girls Who Code was founded with a single mission: to close the gender gap in technology.”

Our mission on campus is to empower female students interested in coding; we want to equip them with all the tools they need to thrive in the field they want to pursue in. That is why in our club, not only do we code projects, but we also aim to introduce our peers to role model women in the computer science, various specific industries within the field through club trips to companies, and inspire them to change the world.

We meet every Monday after school in Mr. Sos’s room (D-30) at Foothill from 3:00-4:30. For more information and updates, please join our facebook page: FHS Girls Who Code!
